News & Events

Universal care Foundation Inc. organizes these outreach events from time to time with the assistance of

volunteers to inform, teach, and educate the recipients on healthy food preparation, basic hygiene, and

other practices to enhance healthy lifestyle changes. Malnutrition is a serious epidemic that is ravaging Imo

State in the Southeastern Region of Nigeria. Experts in food health, including the United Nations have talked

about it. Help is urgently needed from good-spirited people all over the world to help save lives. This has

become a global problem. Please donate to this noble cause.
