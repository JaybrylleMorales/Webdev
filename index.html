Universal care nonprofit has identified the urgent need for immediate

assistance to save the lives of children and adults of poor rural

communities of Imo State in the Southeastern region of Nigeria.

Malnutrition, Diabetes, and Hypertension diseases have reached

epidemic proportions among children and adults respectively in this

region. The causes for this situation, among other factors, include

poverty, illiteracy, ignorance, and wide-scale insecurity. Universal care

is providing outreach on awareness and raw and cooked foods

through our soup kitchen services. We teach them how to prepare

healthy foods. Blood glucose and blood pressure tests are checked

and referrals are made if necessary to appropriate medical clinics.

Please select the donation form for this cause to save a life and the

needy. God bless you abundantly. Thanks.
