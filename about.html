Who We Are

Universal Care Foundation Inc has 501C3 tax exempt status. Our Office is located at 1416 Canadian Geese

Ct. Upper Marlboro Maryland 20774.  We organize outreach community events to help children and adults

who are suffering from malnutrition, hypertension and diabetes in the rural communities of Imo State in

Southeastern Nigeria. These. diseases have reached epidemic proportions in these rural communities due

to extreme poverty, ignorance, illiteracy and wide scale insecurity. We provide uncooked foods, cooked

foods, soup kitchen services and teach them how to use available local foods to prepare healthy meals. We

also teach and encourage them to apply relevant changes to enhance healthy lifestyles. The outreach also

involves enlightenment and screening tests for blood pressure and diabetes.

Please consider supporting our advocacy. You may reach out to us at any time of your convenience. Send us a message .

Universal care foundation Inc. nonprofit has identified the urgent

need for immediate assistance to save the lives of children and

adults of poor rural communities of Imo State in the Southeastern

region of Nigeria.Malnutrition, Diabetes, and Hypertension diseases

have reached epidemic proportions among children and adults

respectively in this region. The causes for this situation, among other

factors, include poverty, illiteracy, ignorance, and wide-scale

insecurity. Universal care is providing outreach on awareness and

raw and cooked foods through our soup kitchen services. We teach

them how to prepare healthy foods. Blood glucose and blood

pressure tests are checked, and referrals are made if necessary to

appropriate medical clinics. Please select the donation form for this

cause to save a life and the needy. God bless you abundantly.
